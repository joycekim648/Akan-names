Akan names

Ghanian people have a unique naming method where children are given names that match the day they were born.The Akan names depend on the childs gender.Do you know your Akan name?Here's an easy way to find y0ur name.

Male Names

  1. Sunday _ kwasi
  2. monday - kwadwo
  3. tuesday-kwabena
  4. wednesday-kwaku
  5. thursday-yaw
  6. friday-kofi
  7. saturday-kwame

Female Names

  1. sunday : akosua
  2. monday : adwoa
  3. tuesday : abenaa
  4. wednesday : akua
  5. thursday : yaa
  6. friday : afua
  7. saturday : ama
